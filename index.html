Comfort

The custom-designed interior offers comfortable long-term accommodation for up to 4 people, including a fully equipped kitchen with gas, diesel and electrity burners. Bathroom offers a comfortable shower, sink and a full size toilet with 25l capacity. Our interiors our tempered by latest air-conditioning and heating systems, naturally including recuperation. They offer up to 2000 litres of personal storage capacity to ensure you will travel in comfort.

Build

We use only the best materials to guarantee rugged construction and flawless service. Our lightweight PU sandwich construction with aluminium elements and high-grade interior materials is ready for years of service.

Chassis

Every XRV vehicle is built on a new original chassis from a world-renowned truck maker. The rugged chassis guarantee enough power and endurance for many years of usage while meeting the highest environmental standards. Every model we use is a four-wheel drive with high ground clearance.

Torsion-free
subframe

We developed this feature specifically for travelling in rough terrain. Our unique torsion-free subframe allows for independent movement of the living box and so creates better surface contact for safer and easier driving.
